For our project we have selected Dataset which gives details about TV Shows and Movies available in OTT Platform Netflix using 11 attributes and 8000+ unique show ids as items

Attributes listed in our Dataset:

1. Type
2. Title
3. director
4. cast
5. country
6. date_added
7. rating
8. released year
9. duration
10.listed_in
11.description
